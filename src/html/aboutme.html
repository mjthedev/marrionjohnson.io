About me

I'm a software developer working from Denver. Solving problems via leetcode has been an interesting challange for me these days, i'd say it gets me out of my comfort zone as a developer, which is a good thing. When i'm not building applications, i spend alot of my time playing guitar. A ready bag is kept for whenever i'm feeling adventurous.

Here you'll really get to know me and what i'm into. There's not enough room on the web to share my entire life but i did the best i could. Enjoy!

What I'm up to

Updated September 27th, 2022

Aside from maintaining my website, i'm currently building two apps. The first one is a full stack e-commerce web app using React, Bootstrap and Postgres. The second one is a chat app using React, NestJS, Socket.IO, WebRTC

I'm currently a member of the Electric Hive Community. The community is a safe space where developers, programmers, data scientists and computer science enthusiasts come together to share common interests and ask questions on relavent topics etc.

Keeping a consistent workout routine isn't easy when the work load swarms in but I make it happen one way or another. Staying in the gym at least 3-4 timeds a week keeps my overall health at optimal levels, which allows me to tackle projects with a clear mind. Between my work schedule and workout routine, finding a nice balance to play my guitar is probably one of my biggest challenges at the moment.
